Greetings, I am Pham Xuan Trung, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at KAIST, under the guidance of Professor Chang D. Yoo. With a passion for pushing the boundaries of technology, my research primarily revolves around the captivating realms of Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence.

Within these domains, my focus spans across several fascinating avenues including Self-supervised Learning, Generative Models, Diffusion Models, Multimodal Learning, Speech Processing, and Natural Language Processing.

I take immense pride in my contributions to the academic community, having disseminated my findings through publications in esteemed venues such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CVPR, ECCV, Advanced Materials, Nano Energy, TCSTV, IEEE Transactions on Broadcasting, and IEEE Access.

Additionally, I actively participate as a discerning reviewer for several prestigious conferences and journals: AAAI 2024, ICLR 2024, CVPR (2023, 2024), ECCV 2024, ICML 2024, NeurIPS (2023, 2024), ACCV 2024, ICASSP 2024, ICCV 2023, Neural Networks (NN) 2023, IEEE Transaction on Multimedia (TMM) 2023.
